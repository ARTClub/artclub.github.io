How does it work?

Complete your profile

Tell us what you'd like, where you live, how many pieces you'd like, at which frequency you'd like us to change your rented pieces.

Show us where you want it

Snap a few pictures of the walls where you want your pieces, give us the general dimensions and a feel of the desired scenery.

Choose among our recommendations

We take it from there. Depending on your preferences and the scenery, we give you a few options to choose from along with the definitive fee.

We deliver and install your pieces

Within a week, you'll get your pieces. We do everything, from hitting in new nails to choosing the actual arrangement.

Enjoy! We'll check back soon

Depending on your preferences, we'll be back with a new tailored selection in 3, 6 or 9 months.
